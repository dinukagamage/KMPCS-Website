About Us

Welcome to KMPCS

Your one-stop destination for a wide range of essential services, all nestled in the picturesque village of Kotapola, within the pristine surroundings of the Morawak Korala, right at the doorstep of the magnificent Sinharaja forest.

Multipurpose Co-operative Society Ltd, we take pride in offering a diverse array of services that cater to the needs of our community. We are more than just a company, we are a trusted partner in your everyday life.

Our main goal is our customer satisfaction.

We offer good after sale service to our customers.

Providing open and honest communication to build trust.

We offer quality goods and services to our customers.

Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our actions.

We have well qualified professional staff to assist you.
