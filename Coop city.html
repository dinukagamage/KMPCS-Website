About us in brief

To provide quality service to all the members of the society in purchasing goods and services from the society, 51 cooperative regions have been established within the jurisdiction of the society. This cooperative of the society has become competitive as the price decider of the goods and services in the local area. Because of this, the villagers have always had the opportunity to get goods and services from the private sector at reasonable prices.
